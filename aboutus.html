Our Story

PestGuard is a student-developed project created by BTech Computer Science Engineering students as part of our academic journey. Designed with a focus on real-world applications, our platform aims to help users identify pests quickly and discover effective, eco-conscious solutions.

We combined our technical knowledge with research in agriculture and pest control to build a solution that's informative, user-friendly, and impactful. Whether it's through symptom descriptions or image-based identification, PestGuard empowers users—especially farmers, gardeners, and homeowners—with the tools to make informed decisions.

This project reflects our commitment to solving practical problems using technology. Every feature has been carefully developed with clarity, functionality, and usability in mind, making PestGuard not just a project, but a step towards smarter pest management.
